# brand-intake

Reads all provided brand and client materials and outputs a structured **Brand Profile** —
the shared context passed into every downstream skill in the pipeline.

---

## Step 1 — Inventory What You've Been Given

Before reading anything, list what the user has provided:

- [ ] Brand strategy / brand guide document
- [ ] Tone of voice or messaging doc
- [ ] Competitor analysis or market positioning doc
- [ ] Existing website URL
- [ ] Other client documents (decks, briefs, proposals)

If nothing has been provided, ask:
> "To get started, please share any brand documents you have — a brand guide, tone of voice doc,
> or existing website URL. Even one is enough to begin."

If a website URL is provided, use web_fetch to scrape the homepage and up to 3 key pages
(About, Product/Service, Contact). Extract copy, headlines, and positioning language.

---

## Step 2 — Extract Brand Signals

Read all provided materials and extract the following:

### Identity
- **Brand name**
- **Tagline(s)** (if any)
- **Category** — what market/industry are they in?

### Positioning
- **What they do** — one clear sentence
- **Who they do it for** — specific audience (demographics, psychographics, behaviors)
- **Why they're different** — differentiator or POV vs. competitors
- **Competitors mentioned** (explicit or implied)

### Voice & Tone
- **3–5 voice adjectives** (e.g. "confident, irreverent, poetic")
- **Tone notes** — how formal? How direct? Any phrases they use often?
- **What to avoid** — anything explicitly called out, or implied by the brand character
- **Example lines** — pull 2–3 verbatim lines that best capture the voice

### Audience
- **Primary audience** — who is the website primarily speaking to?
- **Secondary audience** (if any)
- **Pain points / motivations** — what does this audience care about?

### Offer
- **Core product or service**
- **Key benefits** (not features — benefits)
- **Price positioning** — premium, accessible, enterprise, etc.
- **Primary CTA** — what do they want visitors to do?

### Visual Direction (words only)
- **Visual keywords** — from brand docs or inferred from tone
- **Platform context** — Shopify or general web? (ask user if unclear)
- **Any stated visual references or inspiration**

---

## Step 3 — Flag Gaps

After extraction, list anything missing or unclear:

```
GAPS IDENTIFIED:
- No tone of voice doc provided — voice adjectives inferred from brand guide copy
- Audience description is vague — assumed based on product category
- No CTA confirmed — placeholder used
```

Ask the user to fill critical gaps before proceeding. Non-critical gaps can be flagged and worked around.

---

## Step 5 — Confirm and Hand Off

After outputting the Brand Profile, say:

> "Brand Profile is ready. Review it and let me know if anything needs adjusting —
> especially voice, audience, and the primary CTA. Once you confirm, I'll move into
> site architecture."

**Do not proceed to site-architecture until the user confirms the Brand Profile.**
